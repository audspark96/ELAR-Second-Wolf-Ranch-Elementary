At Home Resources

Online Learning

  • ABC Mouse is a paid program that assists in phonics and reading skills from toddler-3rd grade.

  • Adventure Academy is a paid app for advanced readers. This is appropriate fofr students ready for 3rd grade and above reading.
  • IXL is another paid app that is used nationwide in schools and at home. They offer a piad home learning program.
  • Splash Learn is another paid app that offers help in math and reading.

Printable Worksheets

  • Books to read at home with your child.
  • Teachers Pay Teachers This website is FREE to use for anyone. Anyone can make an account, not just teachers. Use this link to sign up for an account and search for any worksheets your child needs practice in: phonics, comprehension, writing, etc.
  • K5 Learning is a free site, no account is needed to print worksheets.
  • k12 Reader is a free site with printable worksheets.
