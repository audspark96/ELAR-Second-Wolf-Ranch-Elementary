Important Information


LIBRARY
Students will visit the library weekly, however it will be different days every week. Students books should remain in their backpacks at all times to ensure they are able to get new ones.

PE
Students will have PE 2-3 times a week. Please wear closed toed shoes, or pack them in their backpacks so they may participate

Grades are posted in Skyward at the end of each 9 weeks. You may log in to access them here.

WATER BOTTLES: Please provide water bottles with a closing lid- NO STRAWS. We are seeing an increase in spills via cups with straws.

STUDENT ID NUMBERS: Please encourage your child to memorize their student ID number. This is used for lunch and to rent book in the library. This number will remain the same the entirety of their time with GISD.
